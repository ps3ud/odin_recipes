Classic Waffles

Description

This is a recipe for waffles, taking around 25 minutes to complete.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
